About INNOVATIA

We at ABES believe that only learning isn't enough and implementation is one of the essential pillars to completing the cycle of learning.

So, this year at Genero'15 we present to you Innovatia a Hackathon aimed at giving those curious and budding minds a place to get together and Hack away,all the while having fun and making new friends.

I wish I could have attended hackathons as a student. As a mentor, I'm glad I can help the next generation of programmers discover their passions, learn new skills, try out concepts they have learned in class, and build real applications that real people can interact with.

John Britton (GitHub Education Lead)

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Programmers, designers, builders and more come together to learn, build, and share their creations over the course of a few days. Hackathons are not limited to computer science majors — anyone who has an interest in technology and is eager to learn can participate in a hackathon.

Rules

  1. Fresh Code We all start coding at the same time. This is done to keep things fair. Please don’t come in and build on top of previous projects if you want to win.
  2. You can come and go but the all the hacking is to done at the venue.
  3. Final Teams can be decided on the day of the Hackathon but , they need to be finalised atleast before the hackathon commences
  4. Teams will only be allowed to submit their projects within the given time limits , failing to do so , the teams will be automatically disqualified.
